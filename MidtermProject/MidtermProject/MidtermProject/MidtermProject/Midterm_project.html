Sophie, a luxury clothing brand geared towards young people
with an appreciation for construction, comfortability,
and sensuality in their everyday garments, is inspired
by those that came before it, such as, Sandy Liang, MiuMiu,
Moschino, Paloma Wool, and Chanel. Using motifs inspired by girlhood
and womanhood to create clothing that inspires feelings of confidence,
maturity, and elegance, the brand simultaneously feeds one's needs
for all things patterns, textures, and colors. While they began in
producing ready-to-wear garments, the fashion house also specializes
in haute couture, with designs similar to those of Dutch Designer, Iris
Van Herpen, using technology and fabrication to create pieces that are
otherworldly.
